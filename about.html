Brisbane Green Future is a simple and playful user-experience designed to help users learn about the relationship between tree-coverage and urban heat in our city. I hope that these visualizations will in turn help you understand just how big of an impact greenery has on us, and on our surrounding environments. With yearly rising temperatures across the globe, it is important that we ensure that Brisbane's future development is informed by smart urban-planning with sustainability in mind.
